About

Mohamed started out taking engines apart to see how they worked — a diploma and a B.Tech in Automobile Engineering, complete with a project turning waste vegetable oil into fuel. Somewhere along the way, he found out the same instinct — pull a system apart, find the inefficiency, put it back together better — works just as well on a retail floor as it does on a carburettor.

An MBA in Marketing & International Business sealed the shift. Since then he's run mall marketing campaigns in Chennai, managed a 500-customer-a-day retail store for Reliance Trends, and now leads multi-branch operations for a coffee shop chain in Jeddah — building teams, fixing supply chains, and squeezing growth out of every channel he touches.

Off the clock, the engineer becomes a storyteller: writing songs, scripting short films, curating events, and editing it all into shape.
